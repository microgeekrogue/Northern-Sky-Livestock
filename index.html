Our Story

A Family Farm Built
on Passion

Northern Sky Livestock is a family operation run by the Kundingers, dedicated to raising and showing top-quality purebred Simmental cattle. We believe great animals are the result of great care ‚Äî superior genetics, thoughtful management, and a genuine love for the breed.

From our pastures to the show ring, every animal we raise reflects our commitment to excellence. We take pride in breeding cattle that are strong performers both on the farm and in open and junior shows alike.

Our herd is fully registered with the American Simmental Association, and we are always happy to talk cattle with fellow breeders, junior exhibitors, and commercial producers looking to improve their operations.
